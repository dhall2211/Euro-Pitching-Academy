    WHO ARE WE OUR STAFF ADVISORY BOARD

We make players realize their potential!

The European Pitching Academy was established to create a center for pitching development. We believe a positive environment is where a student can excel. We strive to create a setting where students can collaborate with their instructors to maximize their potential.

Through extensive research and dedication, we give our students one of the highest standards of pitching training available. The European Pitching Academy is an independent organization with different affiliates. We value all of our partnerships and strive to make baseball better in all parts of Europe!
